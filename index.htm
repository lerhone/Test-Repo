OUR MISSION

As Archer turns seven, many of us are realizing the importance of quality education first hand with our own children. We wanted to find a way to make a difference. With your help, Archer is working with DonorsChoose.org to help teachers implement important classroom projects.

Archer has selected visual arts projects around the country. Ten teachers have proposed projects and outlined their needs. Find the project that speaks to you, press the 'Like' button and Archer will donate to that project. Share with your network and get them to "Like" as well.
